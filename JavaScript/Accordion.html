Yamaha R1

The Yamaha YZF-R1 is a high-performance sport bike renowned for its advanced technology and powerful engine. Launched in 1998, it features a 998cc inline-4 engine that delivers impressive acceleration and top speeds.

Yamaha R15

The Yamaha YZF-R15 is a popular entry-level sport bike known for its sleek design and impressive performance. Powered by a 155cc, liquid-cooled, single-cylinder engine, it offers a perfect balance of power and fuel efficiency

Yamaha R7

The Yamaha YZF-R7 is a versatile sport bike that blends performance with everyday usability. Powered by a 689cc parallel-twin engine, it delivers a strong mix of power and torque, making it ideal for both track days and street riding.
